## Overview

Nita Merchant is a comprehensive merchant management platform designed to streamline voucher operations and branch administration. This internal dashboard empowers merchants with powerful analytics, intuitive management tools, and operational efficiency features for voucher lifecycle management. Developed during my internship at **PT Nusantara Infrastructure Tbk**
