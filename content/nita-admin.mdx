## Short Explanation

NITA (Nusantara Infrastructure Toll Apps) is application to facilitate toll road users in Makassar. Users can print toll e-receipts, monitor live CCTV at toll gates, check toll rates, monitor traffic around toll areas, and other interesting features. So, NITA Admin is a dashboard for Admin to manage their data and content of application NITA. This application was developed during my internship at PT Nusantara Infrastructure Tbk.
